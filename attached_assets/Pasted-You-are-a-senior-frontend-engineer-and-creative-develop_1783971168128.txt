You are a senior frontend engineer and creative developer. Build a production-ready, immersive fashion website with scroll-controlled video animation.







Your goal is to create a premium, Apple-style scroll experience where the video is controlled entirely by scroll position (scroll scrubbing), not autoplay.



========================

CORE FUNCTIONALITY

========================



1. The video must be frame-synced to scroll:

   - Scrolling DOWN → video moves forward

   - Scrolling UP → video rewinds

   - Use video.currentTime tied to scroll progress



2. The animation must feel:

   - Smooth (no lag or jumps)

   - Cinematic and premium

   - Like a 3D product showcase



3. The page must have enough scroll height (e.g. 300–400vh) to allow smooth scrubbing.



========================

TECH IMPLEMENTATION (STRICT)

========================



- Use vanilla JS OR React (preferred: React + Vite)

- Use GSAP + ScrollTrigger for scroll syncing

- Use requestAnimationFrame for smooth updates

- Preload the video before interaction



IMPORTANT:

- Use scrub: true in ScrollTrigger

- Prevent stuttering by syncing scroll progress → video duration

- Clamp values to avoid overflow



Example logic:

scrollProgress = scrollY / (totalScrollableHeight)

video.currentTime = scrollProgress * video.duration



========================

VIDEO SETUP

========================



- Place video in a sticky container:

  - position: sticky

  - top: 0

  - height: 100vh



- The video should:

  - Fill the screen (object-fit: cover)

  - Be centered

  - Have no controls

  - Be muted
